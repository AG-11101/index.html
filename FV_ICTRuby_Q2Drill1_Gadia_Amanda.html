Emergency Go Bag Essentials



Food & Water


It is suggested to buy canned foods and bottled waters when packing for a go bag, as they usually have a longer shelf life.

Clothes & Towels


Especially duing emergencies it is a must to bring extra clothes and towels incase of getting dirt and rips on your current clothes.

Emergency Plans & Documents


It is important to be prepared with a list of contact numbers, copy of important documents, and floor plans in the bag as this might come in handy later.

First Aid Kit


When hurt or injured a first aid kit shall serve as a temporary fix while waiting for the emrgency services to come.
